Resume / CV

Drop your resume.pdf into the portfolio root folder to enable the download and preview button.

Once added, visitors can view and download it directly from the portfolio.

How to add your resume

  • Export your CV as resume.pdf
  • Place it at the root: /portfolio/resume.pdf
  • Commit & push — GitHub Pages will serve it instantly
  • The "Download Resume" button in the navbar will work automatically
